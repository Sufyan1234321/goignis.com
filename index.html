About Us

GoIGNIS is a team of highly skilled engineers from India specializing in delivering top-notch design and engineering services to US-based MEP companies and consultants. Our expertise spans mechanical, electrical, fire alarm, and protection systems, along with ARC solutions. We are proficient in adhering to industry codes such as NFPA, FM, UL, and local AHJ guidelines.

Our mission is to provide tailored solutions that meet our clients' unique needs. By assigning dedicated remote team members, we enable our clients to focus on core business activities while we manage the technical heavy-lifting, boosting their efficiency and profitability.

We stand out for our commitment to delivering accurate designs on time. GoIGNIS offers cost-effective, innovative solutions without compromising on quality, ensuring professional, timely, and efficient service.

Our goal is to help clients thrive through qualified, affordable remote staffing solutions. Our clients attest to enhanced productivity and faster project completion, setting them apart from competitors. Choose GoIGNIS for your engineering needs and see your business excel.
